Objectives

There are many people facing challenging times, especially those who can’t find all the different services, which they are often the last ones to know about. Despite some privileges, I have walked among these people and I still feel their pain and wish to help.

I want to create a service to help people access and Reach resources and support services, to help in difficult situations. I would make it easier to connect these pieces. More and more mobile devices are becoming available, so I wish to empower the people to find the help they need.

My original vision is a mobile application however I can use this opportunity to test a smaller web page, to start learning about the space. Hopefully it can eventually be a ‘front-end’ part to the service, which could later be used and accessed from a user-friendly mobile interface for people to find help.
